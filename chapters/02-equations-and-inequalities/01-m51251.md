Irrigation is a critical aspect of agriculture, which can expand the
yield of farms and enable farming in areas not naturally viable for
crops. But the materials, equipment, and the water itself are expensive
and complex. To be efficient and productive, farm owners and irrigation
specialists must carefully lay out the network of pipes, pumps, and
related equipment. The available land can be divided into regular
portions (similar to a grid), and the different sizes of irrigation
systems and conduits can be installed within the plotted area.
