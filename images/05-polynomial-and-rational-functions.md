## Figure 1: Cardboard box optimization

**Placement:** Section 5, *Integration*, after the worked example.

**Description:** Two-panel figure.

*Top:* a flat rectangle 30 × 20 with squares of side $x$ marked at each corner. Dashed lines show fold-up boundaries. Annotations indicate the resulting box dimensions: length $30 - 2x$, width $20 - 2x$, height $x$.

*Bottom:* a graph of $V(x) = 4x^3 - 100x^2 + 600x$ on $[0, 10]$. Curve starts at $V(0) = 0$, rises to a peak at approximately $x = 3.92$ (max ~1056), then falls to $V(10) = 0$. Maximum point marked.

**Pedagogical purpose:** The student should notice that the polynomial structure of the volume reflects the geometric structure of the box.

**Style notes:** Width: full text-block.
