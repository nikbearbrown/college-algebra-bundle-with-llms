## Figure 1: Clock minute hand integration

**Placement:** Section 5, *Integration*, after the worked example.

**Description:** Clock face with the minute hand sweeping from 12 to 4 (i.e., 20 minutes). Sector swept is shaded. Annotations: arc length $\frac{10\pi}{3}$ in, sector area $\frac{25\pi}{3}$ in², angle $\frac{2\pi}{3}$ rad.
